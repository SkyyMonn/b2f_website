What It Is

NOT A CLASS.
A SYSTEM.

Group Training at B2F is nothing like a gym class. Every session is coach-led, structurally programmed, and intentionally sequenced to produce measurable results over time.

Small groups. Maximum intensity. Zero wasted reps. Each session builds on the last — your coach tracks your progress and adjusts the load as you grow stronger.

This is collective discipline in action. The energy of a room full of serious people doing serious work — focused entirely on performance.

Who It's For

IS THIS
YOU?

  • You've been training inconsistently and want structure that sticks.
  • You're motivated by others around you pushing hard.
  • You want expert coaching without the cost of personal 1-on-1.

Session Breakdown

WHAT HAPPENS
IN 60 MINUTES.

0–10
min

Dynamic Warm-Up

Joints primed. Nervous system activated. No wasted time — the warm-up is purposeful and directly linked to the session's focus.

10–20
min

Skill / Strength Block

Technique-first loading. Whether it's a calisthenics skill, compound lift, or movement pattern — your coach ensures execution is flawless before intensity climbs.

20–50
min

Main Training Block

High-output work. Structured circuits, supersets, or AMRAP blocks designed around the session's goal. This is where results are forged.

50–60
min

Cool-Down + Debrief

Active recovery, mobility work, and a brief debrief with your coach. Progress noted. Next session previewed. You leave better than you arrived.
