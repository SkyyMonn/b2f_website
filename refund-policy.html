Section 01

Our Commitment and General Position

Built 2 Fly provides premium, results-driven coaching services. Our programs are designed and delivered with significant investment of time, expertise, and resources. Every enrollment is treated as a committed partnership between the client and the coaching team.

We operate a strict but fair refund policy that reflects the premium nature of our services, the costs incurred at the point of onboarding, and the value of the coach's time allocated to each client. This policy is designed to protect both the business and our clients.

We strongly encourage all prospective clients to review program details, ask questions, and attend a trial session where applicable before committing to a full enrollment. Once payment is made, you are confirming your commitment to the program.

Section 02

No-Refund Policy — Core Clause

All payments made to Built 2 Fly are non-refundable as a general rule. This applies to all programs, subscriptions, session packs, and coaching engagements, except as explicitly outlined in Section 3 (Refund Eligibility Summary) and Section 10 (Exceptional Circumstances).

Specifically, the following will not qualify for a refund under any circumstances:

  • Change of mind after payment
  • Failure to attend sessions without valid advance notice
  • Dissatisfaction with results where the client has not adhered to the prescribed program
  • Personal scheduling conflicts or work commitments arising after enrollment
  • Relocation to a different city (for in-person programs; transfer to online coaching may be considered)
  • Partial use of a subscription period
  • Failure to complete the mandatory consent form or onboarding process

By enrolling, you understand and accept that a significant portion of the value of any coaching program is delivered at or before the first session — including program design, assessment, scheduling, and coach allocation — and this investment is not recoverable.

Section 04

Group Training

Group training is sold as a monthly subscription or a fixed-duration session pack. The following terms apply:

  • Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable once the billing period commences
  • Unused sessions within a paid month do not carry over to the following month unless otherwise agreed in writing
  • Early termination of a subscription does not entitle the client to a prorated refund
  • If you are unable to attend due to a verified medical emergency, sessions may be frozen for up to 30 days per calendar year, at our discretion
Section 05

Personal Training

Personal training is sold as a fixed monthly package (12 sessions per month or as otherwise specified). The following terms apply:

  • The full monthly package fee is charged at the start of each month and is non-refundable
  • Sessions missed by the client without 24 hours' advance notice are forfeited
  • Sessions missed by the trainer will be rescheduled at no cost to the client
  • If a client wishes to pause a personal training plan due to verified injury or emergency, a maximum of one (1) billing cycle may be paused per 6-month period, subject to approval
  • Session packs have a fixed validity period. Sessions not used within the validity period are forfeited.
Section 06

Online Coaching

Online coaching is delivered as a monthly subscription including custom workout plans, nutrition guidance, form check reviews, and coach communication. The following terms apply:

  • The monthly coaching fee is due in advance and is non-refundable once the coaching cycle begins
  • A coaching cycle begins on the date your personalised program is delivered or on the 3rd day after payment, whichever is earlier
  • Clients are responsible for engaging with their plan. Inactivity does not entitle a client to an extended cycle or refund.
  • If the client cancels online coaching mid-month, no partial refund is issued
  • If you have not received any program delivery within 7 days of payment, contact us immediately — this will be reviewed
Section 07

Missed Sessions Policy

Sessions are a valuable and time-limited resource. The following rules govern missed or cancelled sessions by the client:

Advance Cancellation

  • Sessions cancelled with at least 24 hours' advance notice may be rescheduled, subject to coach availability
  • Each client is entitled to a maximum of two (2) reschedules per month
  • Rescheduled sessions must be completed within the same billing cycle where possible

Late Cancellation and No-Shows

  • Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice are forfeited and non-refundable
  • Clients who fail to attend a session without notice (no-show) forfeit that session entirely
  • Repeated no-shows (3 or more in a month) may result in suspension of scheduling privileges

Trainer-Side Cancellations

  • If a session is cancelled by Built 2 Fly or your assigned trainer, it will be rescheduled at no cost to you
  • In cases where rescheduling is not possible within the billing cycle, a session credit will be issued
Section 08

Cancellation Terms

If you wish to cancel your enrollment or subscription:

  • Written notice of cancellation must be submitted to us by email at least 7 days before the next billing date
  • Cancellation requests submitted after the next billing date has been charged will apply to the following billing cycle
  • Cancellations do not entitle the client to a refund of fees already paid
  • Your program access remains active until the end of the current billing period

To initiate a cancellation, email us at support@built2fly.com with your full name, phone number, and the subject line "Cancellation Request."

Section 09

Subscription Renewals

All subscriptions renew automatically on their respective billing dates unless cancelled in accordance with Section 8. By enrolling in a subscription:

  • You authorise Built 2 Fly and/or Razorpay to charge the applicable fee to your chosen payment method on each renewal date
  • You are responsible for ensuring your payment method remains valid and funded
  • Failed payments may result in suspension of service access
  • Fees are subject to change with 14 days' prior written notice to the client. Continued enrollment after the effective date of a fee change constitutes acceptance.
Section 10

Exceptional Circumstances

We recognise that genuine exceptional circumstances may arise. Built 2 Fly may, at its sole discretion, consider partial refunds, session credits, or program pauses in the following situations:

  • Serious illness or hospitalisation, supported by a medical certificate from a registered practitioner
  • Death of an immediate family member
  • A program delivery failure on our part that is material, documented, and unresolved after a reasonable notice period
All exceptional requests must be submitted in writing within 7 days of the event. Requests submitted after this period will not be considered. Supporting documentation must accompany all requests. Built 2 Fly's decision in these matters is final and binding.
Section 11

How to Submit a Refund Request

If you believe you are entitled to a refund under the terms outlined above, please submit a request by:

  1. Emailing support@built2fly.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Including your full name, registered phone number, program enrolled in, date of payment, and Razorpay transaction ID
  3. Clearly stating the reason for your request and attaching any supporting documents

We will acknowledge your request within 3 business days and issue a decision within 10 business days. If approved, refunds will be credited to your original payment method within 5–7 business days via Razorpay.

Section 12

Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or card provider without first attempting to resolve the matter directly with Built 2 Fly is considered a breach of this policy and our Terms of Service.

In the event of an unjustified chargeback, we reserve the right to:

  • Immediately suspend or terminate your access to all Built 2 Fly programs and services
  • Submit evidence to the payment processor and/or bank to contest the dispute
  • Pursue recovery of the disputed amount through appropriate legal channels
  • Permanently ban the individual from future enrollment in any Built 2 Fly program

We encourage all clients to contact us directly before escalating any payment concern. Most issues can be resolved promptly through direct communication.
