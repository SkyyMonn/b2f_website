GOT QUESTIONS?

Everything you need to know about our programs, pricing, and how it all works — right here.

Getting Started
Am I too out of shape to join?
+
Absolutely not. B2F is specifically built for beginners and fat loss clients. Every program is scaled to your current fitness level — we assess where you are before designing your plan. You don't need to be fit to start. You just need to start.
How do I get started?
+
Simple. Fill out our application form, tell us about your goals, and we'll be in touch within 24 hours. We'll walk you through program options, pricing, and next steps — no pressure, no hard sell.
What happens during the onboarding process?
+
Once you choose a plan, you'll complete a mandatory onboarding form that covers your fitness history, goals, medical declarations, and consent. After that, your coach will design your custom plan and you'll get access to the B2F client dashboard to begin.
Programs
What programs does Built 2 Fly offer?
+
We offer four core programs: Group Training (small-group sessions), Personal Training (dedicated one-on-one coaching), Online Coaching (custom plans with remote support), and Kids Fitness (age-appropriate group training for children). We also specialise in calisthenics, parkour, and flips training.
Do you offer programs for kids?
+
Yes. Our Kids Fitness program offers age-appropriate group training with specialised trainers. These are structured, fun, and designed to build lifelong healthy habits — not just short-term fitness. Sessions are carefully curated to be safe, engaging, and developmentally appropriate.
Can I switch between programs?
+
Yes. You can upgrade or change your plan at any time. Simply contact your trainer or use the messaging feature in the B2F dashboard and we'll process the switch quickly — usually within the same week.
Pricing & Payment
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We use Razorpay for all transactions — supporting UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and wallets. Payments are fully secure and processed instantly. You'll receive a digital receipt immediately after payment.
Is there a joining or registration fee?
+
No hidden fees. You pay only the monthly plan rate. Personal Training is ₹8,999/month and Online Coaching is ₹5,999/month. Group Training pricing is provided during consultation based on the session schedule.
What is your refund policy?
+
Refunds are subject to our standard policy which is outlined at the time of signup. Generally, refund requests must be submitted within 3 days of the billing date, before the training cycle begins. Contact us directly if you have a specific situation and we'll work with you.
Online Coaching
How does online coaching actually work?
+
You receive custom workout and nutrition plans via the B2F client dashboard. Your coach checks in weekly, reviews your form through video submissions, tracks your metrics, and adjusts your program every month based on your progress. You also get 24/7 WhatsApp support for questions and motivation.
Do I need equipment for online coaching?
+
Not necessarily. During onboarding, we assess what you have access to — gym, home setup, or no equipment at all — and design your program accordingly. Our calisthenics and bodyweight programs are particularly effective with minimal or no equipment.
Policies & Consent
What is the consent form?
+
The consent form is a mandatory digital document completed during onboarding. It covers a liability waiver, medical declaration, payment policy, attendance rules, and optional media consent. It must be completed in full before you can access the B2F client dashboard and begin training.
Is my personal data kept private?
+
Absolutely. Your data is never shared with third parties. Progress photos and videos are only used (with your explicit consent) for internal coaching review. Media is only posted publicly if you've opted into media consent during onboarding. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
