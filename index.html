Questions

GOT
QUESTIONS?

Am I too out of shape to join? +
Absolutely not. B2F is specifically built for beginners and fat loss clients. Every program is scaled to your current fitness level. You don't need to be fit to start — you just need to start.
What is the consent form? +
It's a mandatory digital form completed during onboarding. It covers a liability waiver, medical declaration, payment policy, attendance rules, and optional media consent. It must be completed before accessing the dashboard.
Can I switch between plans? +
Yes. You can upgrade or change your plan at any time. Contact your trainer or reach out via the dashboard and we'll sort it out instantly.
How does online coaching work? +
You receive custom workout and nutrition plans via the client dashboard. Your coach checks in regularly, reviews your form via video, and adjusts your program monthly based on your progress.
Do you offer programs for kids? +
Yes. We offer age-appropriate group training programs for kids with specialised trainers. These are structured, fun, and designed to build lifelong healthy habits.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We use Razorpay for all payments — supporting UPI, cards, net banking, and wallets. Payments are fully secure and processed instantly.
