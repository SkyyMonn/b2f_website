Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect information that is necessary to provide our services, manage your account, and ensure your safety during training. We collect information in the following categories:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Identity information: Full name, date of birth, gender
  • Contact information: Email address, phone number, residential city
  • Health and fitness data: Current fitness level, known injuries or medical conditions, physical limitations, dietary preferences, training history
  • Emergency contact: Name and phone number of a designated emergency contact
  • Payment information: Billing name and address (payment card details are processed directly by our payment gateway and are not stored by us)
  • Media consent: Your decision regarding whether your photographs or videos may be used for promotional purposes
  • Communications: Messages sent to us via contact forms, email, or WhatsApp

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and browser data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent on site, referring URLs, click paths
  • Cookie data: Session tokens, preference settings (see Section 7)

2.3 Information from Third Parties

If you register or log in using a third-party service, we may receive basic profile information from that service (such as name and email). We do not purchase personal data from data brokers.

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Creating and managing your client account and dashboard
  • Delivering training programs, workout plans, nutrition guidance, and coaching services
  • Processing payments and maintaining financial records as required by law
  • Communicating with you about your program, session schedules, and progress
  • Personalising your training experience based on your fitness level, goals, and medical history
  • Ensuring your safety during physical training by understanding pre-existing conditions
  • Sending service-related notifications, program updates, and account information
  • Responding to your queries and support requests
  • Sending promotional communications (only with your explicit consent, and you may opt out at any time)
  • Complying with applicable laws and legal obligations
  • Preventing fraud, enforcing our terms, and protecting the rights of clients and the business

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their marketing purposes.

Section 04

Where applicable under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India) and international frameworks, we process your personal data on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: Where you have given explicit consent (e.g., marketing communications, media use)
  • Contractual necessity: To fulfil our obligations to you as a paying client
  • Legitimate interests: For improving our services, maintaining security, and communicating about your program
  • Legal obligation: Where required by Indian law or regulatory authorities

We may share your information with trusted third parties only to the extent necessary to deliver our services:

  • Payment processors: Razorpay for secure payment transactions (see Section 6)

Section 06

Payment Processing

All payments on the Built 2 Fly platform are processed by Razorpay Software Private Limited, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway licensed by the Reserve Bank of India. When you make a payment:

  • Your card or bank details are entered directly on Razorpay's secure servers
  • Built 2 Fly does not receive, store, or have access to your full card number, CVV, or banking credentials
  • Razorpay's own privacy policy governs the processing of your payment data
  • We retain transaction records (amount, date, reference ID) for accounting and dispute resolution purposes

Razorpay's privacy policy can be reviewed at razorpay.com/privacy.

Section 07

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (session management, security)
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and choices
  • Analytics cookies: Collect anonymised data about how visitors use the site (Google Analytics)
  • Marketing cookies: Used only with your consent to deliver relevant promotions

You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality. Most browsers allow you to refuse new cookies, delete existing ones, or receive a warning before a cookie is set.

Section 08

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law:

  • Active client data: Retained for the duration of your engagement with us
  • Financial records: Retained for a minimum of 7 years in accordance with Indian accounting and tax laws
  • Health and fitness data: Retained for 3 years after your last active session
  • Marketing consent records: Retained until you withdraw consent
  • Legal claims: Data may be retained longer if required for ongoing or potential legal proceedings

When data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymise it in a manner that prevents reconstruction.

Section 09

Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted over our website
  • Access controls ensuring only authorised personnel can access client data
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with encrypted databases
  • Regular security reviews of our systems and third-party integrations
No method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we take every reasonable precaution, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that is likely to affect your rights, we will notify you promptly in accordance with applicable law.
Section 10

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data where it is no longer needed, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Right to restrict processing: Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for marketing or media use at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@built2fly.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

Section 11

Children's Privacy

Built 2 Fly offers fitness programs for minors (under 18 years of age) as part of its Kids Fitness program. For any minor enrolled in our services:

  • A parent or legal guardian must provide consent on behalf of the minor
  • All personal data relating to the minor is collected with parental consent
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children without verified parental authorisation
  • Parents may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their child's data at any time

If you believe we have collected data from a minor without appropriate consent, please contact us immediately at privacy@built2fly.com.
