The System

HOW IT
WORKS.

01
Apply & Onboard
You fill out your application, complete onboarding, and get access to the B2F client dashboard. Your coach reviews your full profile — fitness history, goals, equipment access, and schedule.
02
Receive Your Custom Plan
Your training and nutrition plan is built from scratch and delivered to your dashboard within 48 hours of onboarding. No templates. No guesswork. Every set, rep, and meal intentional.
03
Train, Log & Submit
Follow your program. Log your sessions in the dashboard. Submit form videos for your coach to review. Every week, your data builds a clearer picture of your progress.
04
Weekly Check-In & Adjustment
Your coach reviews your logs, form videos, and metrics every week. The plan is adjusted based on what the data shows — not assumptions. You're never on a static program for long.
05
Monthly Strategy Call
A full review call with your coach every month. Body composition update, goal realignment, and a roadmap for the month ahead. You always know where you're going and why.
