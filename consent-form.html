Acknowledgment of Inherent Risks
Physical training carries inherent risks that cannot be entirely eliminated.

I acknowledge and accept that participation in any Built 2 Fly training program — including but not limited to group training, personal training, online coaching, calisthenics instruction, skill-based training, mobility work, and prehab programming — involves physical exertion and carries inherent risk, including:

  • Muscle soreness, fatigue, and temporary physical discomfort
